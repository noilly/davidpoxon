About

Welcome, it's so great to have you here.

My name is David and I was born and raised in Brisbane, Australia.

My main passions are software engineering and entrepreneurism. This is why I find myself simultaneously studying for a PhD in computer science and founding a startup technology company. My research is in a fascinating area of information theory known as 'information flow control', which is a formalisation of the question, "how do we control the propagation of information after it has been released?" My startup, Velvet™, is creating the Wi-Fi sharing economy.

My other passions include productivity hacking, burgers and simplicity.
